Pato no tucupi

Ingredients

  • 1 duck (3 kilograms)
  • 3 liters of tucupi
  • 5 packs of jambu
  • 10 cloves of garlic
  • 2 onions
  • 1 pack of chicory
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 lemon
  • 1 pinch of black pepper
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 2 spoons of olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chilli pepper to taste

Method

The Duck

  1. Clean the duck and wash it with a few leaves of chicory and lemon
  2. Leave to mix overnight in vinegar with garlic, salt and pepper
  3. Cut in to pieces and brown it with onion, garlic, tomato (finely chopped), butter and olive oil
  4. Cook in a pan until it's soft
  5. Put

The tucupi

  1. Cook the Tucupi with freshly crushed whole garlic salt oil and chicory

The jambu

  1. Clean it well, remove all the flowers, cook with water and salt until softened, unstick and add to Tucupi

The spicy sauce

  1. Put some hot tucupi in a saucepan, add the lightly crushed red pepper
  2. When the duck is cooked, add it to a single pan with the tucupi and the jambu
  3. Serve with Rice Mandioca Flour
